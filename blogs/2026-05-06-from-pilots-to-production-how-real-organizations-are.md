There’s a well-known gap at the heart of enterprise AI right now: 71% of organizations are deploying AI agents, yet only 11% of those use cases reached production last year. Boards are demanding ROI from AI investments, but most leadership teams are still pointing to pilots. That gap is exactly what CamundaCon 2026 plans to address during our sessions.
